Let us re-introduce ourselves
AOK Supply has relaunched as
inPractice

It is safe to say that the pandemic has changed many aspects of life and brought overlooked truths, both challenging and necessary to the forefront. Running our small business has been no different. As Sean, Joanna and I have evolved so too has our business.

To that end we are scaling down our work as OK Studio, a small design agency we have run parallel to AOK Supply, and relaunching AOK Supply as In Practice, a modern home goods company built on sustainability, community and beauty.

We’re on a mission to reimagine our relationship to daily living through accessible rituals grounded in simple virtues. As we dive into this next chapter we intend to put into practice through actions like donating to orgs like My Sistah’s House through the Floret Coalition, applying for B-Corp Status, sharing Conscious Counsel Interviews (see our conversation with herbalist and artist AnAkA below) and as always designing sustainable and intentional products. Thank you for your continued support, we’d love for you to join us as we work imperfectly towards perfection together.

-Grant
